Features
Everything you need to
track smarter

Built around the way you actually spend — with AI to save time and analytics to give you insight.

📷
AI Receipt Scanning
Snap a photo of any receipt. AI extracts the outlet, amount, date and category — with confidence indicators so you know what to verify.
📊
Rich Analytics
6 chart types including Paid vs Actual Price comparison, day-of-week patterns, cumulative spend, and top outlets — switchable by month, quarter or year.
🔄
Real-time Sync
Add an expense on your phone, see it instantly on your laptop. Works offline too — syncs automatically when you're back online.
📥
CSV Import
Already tracking in Excel? Import your existing data in seconds. Auto-maps your column headers including Date, Month, Day, Item, Category, Outlet, Paid Price and Actual Price.
💰
Savings Tracking
Track both Paid Price and Actual Price to see exactly how much you're saving through discounts, loyalty points and promotions every month.
📱
Mobile First
Swipe between pages like a native app. Install to your home screen on Android or iPhone — no app store needed.
